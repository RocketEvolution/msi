Crystal Style is a multi-disciplinary design studio dedicated to exploration of function, detail, and character in our work.


We are proud to bring the human element, genuineness, appreciation of material, and the inclusive spirit that grows from our Brooklyn roots to our collaborative design process. We design and create at many scales, from furniture and products; to residential interiors, branded environments, and public art installations.


We are known for our love of color and material palettes; playful yet carefully resolved compositions; meaningful visual motifs; and modern forms. We enjoy embarking on design challenges together with our clients and customers! We design solutions for better living, learning, and community.
